# Reaction Tester
 
A browser-based reaction time tester made with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. The game measures how fast you can respond to a box that changes color. You press spacebar as fast as you can and your reaction time will be recorded in milliseconds.
 
## How It Works
 
When the page loads you are shown the rules. Press spacebar to start. A purple box will appear in the center of the screen and after a short amount of time, the box will turn yellow. Press spacebar as fast as you can when you see the box change colors and your reaction time will be displayed. Your best time is saved locally in your browser using local storage.
